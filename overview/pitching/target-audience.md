## Tailoring Checklist

When creating content for a specific audience:

- [ ] Identified which audience segment(s)
- [ ] Emphasized concerns that matter to them
- [ ] Used appropriate language and terminology
- [ ] Focused on relevant benefits and outcomes
- [ ] Aligned with their decision-making criteria
- [ ] Matched tone to audience expectations
