# Market - Context Guide

## Purpose

This area contains market analysis, competitive landscape, and positioning information for CABAL. Use these files when making strategic decisions, pitching to investors, or understanding the market context.

## Quick Navigation

- **Start here:** [positioning.md](./positioning.md) - How CABAL is positioned in the market
- **For competition:** [competitive-landscape.md](./competitive-landscape.md) - Competitors and differentiation
- **For market sizing:** [market-analysis.md](./market-analysis.md) - Target market and opportunity size

## File Descriptions

### positioning.md
**When:** Making strategic decisions, pitching to investors/partners
**What:** CABAL's market positioning, target segments, and positioning strategy

Use when you need to understand or communicate where CABAL fits in the market.

### competitive-landscape.md
**When:** Understanding competitors, making differentiation decisions
**What:** Analysis of competitors, alternatives, and CABAL's unique advantages

Use when you need to understand competitive dynamics or articulate differentiation.

### market-analysis.md
**When:** Strategic planning, investor pitches, growth planning
**What:** Market size, trends, target segments, and opportunity assessment

Use when you need market data to inform decisions or make the business case.

**Explore when:**
- Pitching to investors or partners

- Planning growth or go-to-market strategy

**Read positioning.md when:**
