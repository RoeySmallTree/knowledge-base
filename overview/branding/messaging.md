## Message Testing

### Does your message work?

Checklist for evaluating messaging:

- [ ] Clear: Is it immediately understandable?
- [ ] Concise: Can you say it in fewer words?
- [ ] Compelling: Does it make someone care?
- [ ] Credible: Can you back it up?
- [ ] Consistent: Does it align with brand voice?
- [ ] Differentiated: Could only CABAL say this?
