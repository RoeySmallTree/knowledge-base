# CABAL VITALS

> *"The Asset is You."*

## District Overview

**CABAL VITALS** is the personal life optimization district. While ARCADE entertains and OPERATIONS delivers professional work, VITALS focuses on **you**—health, wealth, relationships, goals, and personal development.

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **District Code** | VITALS |
| **Slogan** | "The Asset is You." |
| **Team Count** | 12 |
| **Primary Purpose** | Personal development, life optimization |
| **Target Users** | Everyone—personal life is universal |

---

## Philosophy

> "Most AI tools help you work. VITALS helps you **live**."

### Design Principles
1. **Personal, Not Generic** - YOUR context, not population averages
2. **Systems, Not Tips** - Sustainable frameworks, not one-off advice
3. **Judgment-Free** - No shame, just optimization
4. **Holistic Integration** - Teams can cross-reference
5. **Progress Over Perfection** - Celebrate improvement
