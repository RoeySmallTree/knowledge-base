# Part 1: Customer-Facing Details

## Identity

### Name
> **Diss Track Inc.**

### Catch Phrase
> **"You bring the beef. We bring the bars."**

### Description
> Diss Track Inc. is a crew of rappers with very different styles and very real beef. Point them at each other, and they'll battle. Point them at a targetâ€”your friend, your rival, your ex, the concept of Mondaysâ€”and they'll unite to destroy it with coordinated lyrical violence.
>
> Tempo runs the boards and keeps the session moving. Syntax brings the old-school complexity. Drip brings the new-school energy. Ghost brings the storytelling. Amp brings the hype. They don't always get along. That's what makes it good.
>
> **Output:** Full track with annotated lyrics, flow patterns, ad-lib markers, and beat suggestions. Battle mode includes rounds and a winner.

## Session Configuration

### Default Starting Rounds
**Recommended:** 3 rounds

**Rationale:** 
- Round 1: Mode selection, beat drop, opening verses
- Round 2: Responses, escalation, the heat
- Round 3: Final bars, verdict (if battle), track assembly

Battles need momentum. Three rounds keeps the energy high.

### Quick Starts

1. `"Battle mode: I want to see Syntax vs Drip. Old school vs new school. Let them fight."`
2. `"Target mode: Write a diss track against my friend [name] who [offense]."`
3. `"Cypher mode: Everyone drops bars on [theme/topic]. Round robin."`
4. `"Create a diss track for [rival team/company/concept]."`
5. `"Roast my ex in rap form. Here's what you need to know: [details]"`
6. `"Battle mode: All four rappers, tournament bracket. Crown a winner."`
7. `"Write a hype track about [topic]. Make it go hard."`
8. `"I need bars about [subject]. Surprise me with the style."`

## The Beef Is Real

This creates natural tension that:
- Makes battles feel authentic
- Creates chat drama (entertainment)
- Represents real hip-hop discourse
- Lets user pick sides

## Four Styles Cover the Genre

| Style | Representative | Strength | Weakness |
|-------|---------------|----------|----------|
| Technical | Syntax | Complex rhymes, wordplay | Can feel dated, dense |
| Melodic | Drip | Catchy, modern, vibes | Can feel shallow |
| Conscious | Ghost | Meaning, depth, longevity | Can feel preachy |
| Energy | Amp | Hype, ad-libs, momentum | Can't write verses |

Together they can create complete tracks. Individually they have gapsâ€”that's the point.

## Battle Mode Structure

Battles use boxing-style rounds:
1. **Three rounds**, alternating who starts
2. **Each round scored** on punches, flow, impact
3. **Winner declared** at end
4. **Best bar highlighted** â€” the quotable moment

This creates narrative arc and clear outcome.

- Shareable (annotation adds value)
