> - Org assessment and restructuring
> - Role clarity and RACI matrices
> - Hiring plans and recruitment strategy
> - Compensation benchmarking and equity
> - Culture assessment and improvement
> - Performance management systems
> - Leadership development
>

> - Org charts and reporting structures
> - Complete job descriptions and role specs
> - Compensation and benefits frameworks
> - Hiring plans and interview processes
> - Onboarding playbooks

# Part 2: Rationale & Considerations (Internal)

## Critical Design: Build vs. Improve

**The Insight:** Organizations have two fundamental needs:
1. **BUILD** — Create organizational systems from nothing (startups, new divisions)
2. **IMPROVE** — Fix, optimize, or scale existing systems (growth, restructuring)

The team adapts its approach based on whether we're building greenfield or improving existing.

**Build Mode:**
```
VISION → STRUCTURE → ROLES → PEOPLE → CULTURE → SYSTEMS
   │         │         │        │         │         │
   ▼         ▼         ▼        ▼         ▼         ▼
What do   How does   What     Who do   How do   How do
we want   work get   jobs     we      we work   we
to be?    done?      exist?   hire?   together? operate?
```

**Improve Mode:**
```
ASSESS → DIAGNOSE → DESIGN → IMPLEMENT → SUSTAIN
   │         │          │         │          │
   ▼         ▼          ▼         ▼          ▼
What do   What's     What      How do    How do
we have?  broken?    should    we get    we keep
                     it be?    there?    it?
```

---

## Stage-Appropriate Design

Different organizational stages need different approaches:

| Stage | Employees | Focus | Formality Level |
|-------|-----------|-------|-----------------|
| **Founding** | 1-5 | Role clarity, culture DNA, first hires | Low (flexible) |
| **Early** | 5-20 | Structure basics, hiring process, comp philosophy | Medium |
| **Growth** | 20-100 | Departments, management layers, systems | Medium-High |
| **Scale** | 100-500 | Process, specialization, culture preservation | High |
| **Enterprise** | 500+ | Optimization, governance, strategic HR | Very High |

The team calibrates formality and complexity to the organization's stage.

# Part 5: Key Design Decisions

## Build vs. Improve Modes

The team adapts approach based on:
- **BUILD:** Creating from scratch for new orgs
- **IMPROVE:** Fixing/optimizing existing orgs

Both are valid, both need different approaches.

## Stage-Appropriate Design

A 5-person startup doesn't need enterprise HR. The team calibrates complexity to stage:
- Founding: Minimal structure, strong culture DNA
- Early: Basic systems, clear roles
- Growth: More formal processes
- Scale: Full HR infrastructure

## Culture Is Operational

Values aren't inspirational posters. Every value includes:
- What it means
- Behaviors that demonstrate it
- Behaviors that violate it
- How to hire for it

If you can't operationalize it, it's not a real value.

## RACI Prevents Conflict

Role clarity prevents 90% of organizational conflict. RACI matrices make explicit:
- Who decides
- Who does the work
- Who provides input
- Who needs to know

Clarity is kindness.
