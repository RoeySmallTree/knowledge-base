# The Agency

> *"Crack any market for any product."*

## Rational
Full-stack marketing team producing complete campaign strategies. Simulates top creative agencies—strategy, creative, media planning—delivering campaign-in-a-box.

## Premise
A creative agency team develops comprehensive marketing campaigns from strategy through execution specs.

## Expected Outputs

1. **Creative Brief** - Objectives, audience, key messages
2. **Campaign Concept** - The big idea with rationale
3. **Copy Deck** - Headlines, body copy, CTAs
4. **Media Plan** - Channels, budget, timing, KPIs
5. **Asset List** - What needs production
6. **Measurement Framework** - Success tracking
