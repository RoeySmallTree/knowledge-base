# The Incubator

> *"Complete startup blueprint in hours, not months."*

## Rational
Transforms vague startup ideas into rigorous business blueprints. Simulates top accelerators—Y Combinator, Techstars—condensing months of refinement into structured output.

## Premise
A team of startup specialists takes rough ideas and produces complete, fundable business plans with technical architecture, GTM strategy, and investor-ready materials.

## Expected Outputs

1. **Lean Canvas** - One-page business model
2. **Technical Architecture** - Stack, costs, timeline
3. **GTM Strategy** - Channels, messaging, milestones
4. **18-Month P&L** - Revenue, costs, funding needs
5. **Investment Memo** - Why this could work (or not)
6. **FAQ** - Anticipated investor questions

## Sample Prompts

- "I want to build an AI-powered X for Y market"
- "Evaluate this startup idea: [description]"
- "Help me prepare for Y Combinator application"
- "I have $50K and a technical co-founder—what can we build?"
