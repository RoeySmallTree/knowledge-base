# The Studio

> *"Perfect continuity from first draft to final cut."*

## Expected Outputs

1. **Script Bible** - Series overview, characters, world, tone
2. **Season Arc** - Episode breakdown with beats
3. **Pilot Script** - Formatted teleplay
4. **Character Profiles** - Background, voice, arc
5. **Continuity Document** - Reference for consistency
