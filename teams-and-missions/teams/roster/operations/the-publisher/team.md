## Expected Outputs

1. **Book Proposal** - Concept, audience, positioning
2. **Chapter Outline** - Full structure with summaries
3. **Character/Voice Bible** - Consistency reference
4. **Draft Chapters** - Written content
5. **Editorial Notes** - Feedback and revisions
