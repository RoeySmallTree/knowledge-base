# RFP Response Unit (Cortex)

> *"Turn 100-page RFP into 50-page proposal with gaps surfaced."*

## Overview

The Cortex RFP Response Unit combines knowledge management with proposal generation. It ingests RFPs, maps requirements to capabilities, and generates compliant proposals while surfacing what's missing.

## Tags
`#cortex` `#product` `#compliance` `#rfp` `#proposals`

## Use Cases
- RFP response
- Proposal generation
- Capability gap analysis
- Compliance mapping
- Bid management

## Composition

| Role | Agent | Function |
|------|-------|----------|
| Chair | Capture Manager | Win strategy and proposal leadership |
| Operative | Technical Writer | Response drafting and content creation |
| Operative | Commercial Lead | Pricing and terms development |
| Watchdog | Compliance Officer | Requirements compliance and gap flagging |

## Expected Outputs
1. **Proposal** — Complete RFP response
2. **Compliance Matrix** — Requirements mapped to responses
3. **Gap Analysis** — Missing capabilities or information
4. **Win Strategy** — Differentiation approach

- "Generate a proposal matching our capabilities to their requirements"
- "What gaps do we have for this RFP?"
