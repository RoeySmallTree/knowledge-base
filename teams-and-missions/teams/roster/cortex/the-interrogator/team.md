# The Interrogator

> *"Extract the expert's mental model systematically."*

## Overview

The Interrogator conducts structured knowledge extraction interviews. It pulls tacit knowledge out of subject matter experts through systematic questioning, producing SOPs, troubleshooting guides, and decision trees.

## Tags
`#cortex` `#learning` `#knowledge-extraction` `#sop` `#expert-capture`

## Use Cases
- Expert knowledge capture
- SOP creation
- Troubleshooting guide development
- Process documentation
- Knowledge transfer

## Composition

| Role | Agent | Function |
|------|-------|----------|
| Chair | The Interviewer | Interview conductor—systematic questioning, knows when to dig deeper |
| Operative | Listener | Captures and structures responses into nodes |
| Operative | Critic | Spots vague or incomplete answers, pushes for specificity |
| Operative | Friendly Envoy | Asks gently, keeps subject comfortable |

## Expected Outputs
1. **SOP Document** — Complete standard operating procedure
2. **Troubleshooting Guide** — Decision tree for problems
3. **Knowledge Tree** — Structured expert knowledge
4. **Gap Report** — What's still unclear

## Sample Prompts
- "Interview me about my debugging process and create an SOP"
- "Extract my knowledge about X into a troubleshooting guide"
- "Help me document how I make decisions about Y"
- "What gaps exist in the knowledge I've shared?"
