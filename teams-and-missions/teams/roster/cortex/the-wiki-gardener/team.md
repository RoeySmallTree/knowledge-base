# The Wiki Gardener

> *"Sanitation crew for your knowledge chaos."*

## Overview

The Wiki Gardener transforms chaotic information dumps into organized, maintained knowledge bases. The team that takes scattered docs, Slack messages, and meeting notes and turns them into a pristine wiki.

## Tags
`#cortex` `#knowledge-management` `#documentation` `#wiki` `#organization`

## Use Cases
- Documentation cleanup
- Knowledge base creation
- Onboarding documentation
- Process documentation
- Information architecture

## Composition

| Role | Agent | Function |
|------|-------|----------|
| Chair | The Librarian | Information architect—owns structure and findability |
| Operative | Scribe | Rewrites raw content into clean, consistent prose |
| Operative | Linker | Creates cross-references, identifies related content |
| Watchdog | Deprecation Officer | Flags outdated content, identifies conflicts |

## Expected Outputs
1. **Structured Wiki** — Clean, organized knowledge base
2. **Changelog Digest** — What's been updated
3. **Deprecation Queue** — Content needing review
4. **Link Map** — Visualization of content connections

## Sample Prompts
- "Transform these meeting notes into proper documentation"
- "Organize our scattered docs into a searchable wiki"
- "What content is outdated and needs updating?"
- "Create a navigation structure for our knowledge base"
