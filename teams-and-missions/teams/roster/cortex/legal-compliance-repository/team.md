# Legal & Compliance Repository

> *"Flag when laws change that affect your contracts."*

## Overview

The Legal & Compliance Repository maintains a living legal knowledge base with active monitoring for regulatory changes that affect existing documents.

## Tags
`#cortex` `#product` `#compliance` `#legal` `#contracts`

## Use Cases
- Contract management
- Regulatory compliance
- Policy maintenance
- Legal knowledge management
- Compliance monitoring

## Composition

| Role | Agent | Function |
|------|-------|----------|
| Chair | General Counsel | Legal strategy and repository ownership |
| Operative | Regulatory Monitor | Tracks law and regulation changes |
| Operative | Precedent Tracker | Maintains case law and precedent library |
| Operative | Jurisdiction Expert | Multi-jurisdiction knowledge |
| Watchdog | Watchdog | Flags outdated documents and compliance gaps |

## Expected Outputs
1. **Contract Library** — Version-controlled contracts
2. **Compliance Policies** — Current policy documentation
3. **Regulatory Calendar** — Upcoming changes and deadlines
4. **Precedent Library** — Relevant case law
5. **Audit Trail** — Compliance history

## Sample Prompts
- "What regulations have changed that affect our contracts?"
- "Is this clause still compliant with current laws?"
- "Track regulatory changes for our industry"
- "What precedents apply to this situation?"
