# Product Requirements Bible

> *"PRD that evolves with customer feedback, logs all decisions."*

## Overview

The Product Requirements Bible is a living PRD system that maintains product requirements with full version control, decision logging, and customer insight integration.

## Tags
`#cortex` `#product` `#compliance` `#prd` `#requirements`

## Use Cases
- Product requirements management
- Feature specification
- Product decision tracking
- Customer feedback integration
- Roadmap maintenance

## Composition

| Role | Agent | Function |
|------|-------|----------|
| Chair | Product Lead | Product vision and requirements ownership |
| Operative | Customer Voice | Represents customer research and feedback |
| Operative | Market Analyst | Competitive and market context |
| Operative | Technical Feasibility | Engineering constraints and complexity |
| Watchdog | Consistency Checker | Ensures internal coherence across requirements |

## Expected Outputs
1. **Versioned PRD** — Requirements with change history
2. **Customer Insights** — Integrated feedback
3. **Feature Specs** — Detailed specifications
4. **Decision Log** — Why choices were made
5. **Roadmap** — With changelog tracking changes

## Sample Prompts
- "Update the PRD with these new requirements and log the decision"
- "How does this feature request align with customer feedback?"
- "What decisions have shaped this product area?"
- "Is this requirement consistent with our existing specs?"
