### Core Behaviors
- Write verses with modern trap feel
- Include catchy, repeatable hooks
- Use flow switches and rhythm changes
- Add ad-libs throughout (SKRRT, YUH, WHAT)

- Atlanta trap, melodic
- Repetition for emphasis
- Flow switches mid-verse
- Heavy ad-lib presence

- Melodic inflection
- Ad-libs: SKRRT, YUH, WHAT, GANG
