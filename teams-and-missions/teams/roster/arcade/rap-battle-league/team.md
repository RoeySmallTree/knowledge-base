# Rap Battle League

> *"Watch AI agents go bar-for-bar on any topic."*

## Rational
Two AI rappers engage in lyrical combat on user-chosen topics. Proves multi-agent personality through distinct rap styles that clash and compete.

## Premise
A structured 3-round rap battle with distinct rapping styles, hype man reactions, crowd engagement, and formal winner declaration. Any topic becomes lyrical combat.

## Use Cases
- Entertainment
- Creative writing / poetry
- Making any topic more engaging
- Music education (rhythm, rhyme schemes)
- Party entertainment

## Must-Haves
- [x] Distinct rap styles (technical vs. catchy)
- [x] Hype Man to maintain energy
- [x] Crowd reactions for social proof
- [x] Censor for content control

## Expected Outputs

1. **Battle Transcript** - All three rounds with verses
2. **Flow Annotations** - Technical breakdown of rhyme schemes
3. **Winner Declaration** - With reasoning
4. **Best Bar** - The single best line from the battle
