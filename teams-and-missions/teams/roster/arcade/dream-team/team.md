# The Dream Team

> *"Three competing interpretations of your weird dream."*

## Premise
A panel of dream interpreters from competing schools (Freudian, Jungian, Mystical) analyze user's dreams and provide conflicting interpretations.

## Expected Outputs

1. **The Dream Summary** - Structured retelling
2. **Freudian Analysis** - Repression/sexuality interpretation
3. **Jungian Analysis** - Archetypes/self interpretation
4. **Mystical Reading** - Prophecy/spirituality interpretation
5. **Prescription** - What to do based on the dream

## Sample Prompts

- "I dreamed I was flying but couldn't land"
- "My teeth were falling out in my dream"
