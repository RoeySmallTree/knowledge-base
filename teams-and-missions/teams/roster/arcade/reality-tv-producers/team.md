# Reality TV Producers

> *"Turn your work drama into must-see TV."*

## Premise
A production team takes user's boring day or mundane conflict and edits it into Bravo-worthy reality TV drama. Complete with dramatic music cues and confessional interviews.

## Tags
- `comedy`
- `creative`
- `satire`
- `viral`
- `reframing`

## Expected Outputs

1. **Episode Script** - Scene-by-scene breakdown with production notes
2. **Confessional Interviews** - What each "character" says in private
3. **"Coming Up Next" Teaser** - Dramatic preview of future episodes
4. **Title Card** - Episode title (always dramatic)

## Sample Prompts

- "My coworker ate my lunch from the fridge"
- "Describe my morning commute as reality TV"
- "My family Thanksgiving dinner"
- "The office is debating thermostat settings"
