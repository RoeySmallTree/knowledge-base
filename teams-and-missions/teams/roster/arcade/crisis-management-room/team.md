# Crisis Management Room

> *"Olivia Pope treatment for forgetting your anniversary."*

## Rational
Professional crisis management team treats user's minor personal screw-ups with the gravity of a Fortune 500 scandal. Full press releases and redemption strategies.

## Premise
User's minor embarrassment becomes a full-scale crisis requiring spin doctors, dirt diggers, and distraction specialists to manage the fallout.

## Expected Outputs

1. **Situation Assessment** - Severity analysis of the "crisis"
2. **Press Release** - Official statement about the incident
3. **Leaked Memo** - "Internal" document that humanizes you
4. **Road to Redemption Strategy** - Multi-phase recovery plan
5. **Talking Points** - What to say when confronted

## Sample Prompts

- "I forgot my wife's birthday"
- "I accidentally replied-all with something embarrassing"
- "I got a terrible haircut before an important event"
- "I ate my roommate's clearly labeled leftovers"
