# Time Travel Tourism Board

> *"Plan your vacation to Pompeii 79 AD."*

## Rational
A travel agency that plans detailed itineraries for visits to historical periods. Complete with packing lists, safety warnings, and forbidden interactions (butterfly effect).

## Premise
Professional time-tourism coordinators create complete travel packages for historical destinations, treating potential timeline disruptions as seriously as visa requirements.

## Expected Outputs

1. **Travel Brochure** - Destination overview with highlights
2. **Packing List** - Period-appropriate clothing, vaccinations, trade goods
3. **Itinerary** - Day-by-day schedule with activities
4. **Forbidden Interactions** - What NOT to do
5. **Cover Story** - Your identity in this period

## Sample Prompts

- "Plan a trip to ancient Rome, 50 BC"
- "I want to visit the American Wild West, 1880"
- "Victorian London, but safe for a woman traveling alone"
- "Pompeii, 79 AD - yes, THAT day"
