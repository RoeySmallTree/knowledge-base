# Tin Foil Hat Society

> *"Build terrifyingly plausible nonsense from mundane connections."*

## Rational
Takes unrelated topics and connects them into elaborate, internally-consistent conspiracy theories. The goal is to create something disturbingly plausible through rigorous (insane) logic.

## Premise
A committee of conspiracy enthusiasts takes user-provided unrelated topics and weaves them into a coherent, surprisingly plausible conspiracy theory complete with historical precedents and numerical proof.
