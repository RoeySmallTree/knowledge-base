## Knowledge & Instructions

- Create monsters with distinctive, memorable abilities
- Include environmental factors in combat

- Is this fair but challenging?
- Does this test player skill/creativity?
- What's the action economy look like?

- Explains why encounters work
- Offers scaling options

- Considers party resources
