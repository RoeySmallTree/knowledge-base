## Key Takeaways for CABAL Integration

1. **GLM-4.7** is the current flagship - excellent for coding-heavy orchestration tasks
2. **GLM-4.6V** is best for any vision/multimodal workflows in your team compositions
3. **GLM-4.5 Air** offers exceptional cost-efficiency for high-volume operations
4. All models support **MIT licensing** - full commercial use and self-hosting permitted
5. Native **tool calling** support makes them excellent for agentic architectures
6. Strong **Chinese-English bilingual** capabilities across the lineup
7. Pricing is **5-10x cheaper** than comparable Western models (Claude, GPT)
8. "GLM-4.6 Exacto" requires verification - no public documentation found
