## ‚öôÔ∏è Workflow & Mechanics ("The Engine")
**Patterns that determine how work moves, processes, and evolves.**

### ‚úÖ Relay (Sequential Transformation)
- **Folder**: `relay-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Work passes through specialists sequentially, each transforming it
- **Key Use**: Multi-stage production

### ‚úÖ Decompose-Delegate-Synthesize (DDS)
- **Folder**: `dds-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Break problem down, execute in parallel, synthesize back together
- **Key Use**: Complex problem solving

### ‚úÖ Parallel Tracks
- **Folder**: `parallel-tracks-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Independent workstreams that eventually integrate at checkpoints
- **Key Use**: Large projects with distinct components

## ‚öñÔ∏è Quality & Judgment ("The Conscience")
**Patterns that define, measure, and enforce what "good" looks like.**

### ‚úÖ Persistent Adversary
- **Folder**: `persistent-adversary-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Continuous role dedicated to challenging and finding flaws
- **Key Use**: Red teaming, quality control, checking for "bad" output

### ‚úÖ Judgment Panel
- **Folder**: `judgment-panel-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Multiple independent judges evaluate, then deliberate to verdict
- **Key Use**: Reviews, audits, determining "good" vs "bad"

### ‚úÖ Phase Gate (Structured Approval)
- **Folder**: `phase-gate-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Formal user approval requirements between project phases
- **Key Use**: Ensuring quality before proceeding, client sign-off

### ‚úÖ Ensemble (Aggregate Judgment)
- **Folder**: `ensemble-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Aggregate multiple independent judgments to reduce error
- **Key Use**: Reducing hallucination, finding the "best" answer

### ‚úÖ Calibration (Warmup Alignment)
- **Folder**: `calibration-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Warmup phase to establish voice and style standards before main work
- **Key Use**: Quality alignment, setting the bar

### ‚úÖ Referencing Pattern
- **Folder**: `referencing-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Explicit dependency relationships between products (depends_on, derived_from, supersedes)
- **Key Use**: Change propagation, source tracing, coherence validation

### ‚úÖ Race Pattern
- **Folder**: `race-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Multiple operatives solve same problem; resolve by selecting, synthesizing, or evolving
- **Key Use**: Competitive quality, brainstorming, dialectic debate

## üé≠ Experience & Vibe ("The Soul")
**Patterns that shape the personality, tone, and feeling of the interaction.**

### ‚úÖ Deep Immersion (Belief-Level Character)
- **Folder**: `deep-immersion-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Agent IS the character (consciousness layer), not playing a role
- **Key Use**: Entertainment, therapy, simulation

### ‚úÖ Dramatic Process
- **Folder**: `dramatic-process-pattern/`
- **Essence**: The work is real, but the *performance* of doing it is the entertainment
- **Key Use**: Villain councils, board rooms

### ‚úÖ Reactive World
- **Folder**: `reactive-world-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Cycle of User Action ‚Üí World Reaction; products represent state
- **Key Use**: Games, simulations, interactive fiction

### ‚úÖ Ensemble Stage
- **Folder**: `ensemble-stage-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Multiple performers contribute to a shared "show"
- **Key Use**: Roasts, talent shows, panels

### ‚úÖ Exchange (Turn-Based Performance)
- **Folder**: `exchange-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Back-and-forth moves between performers; the exchange is the output
- **Key Use**: Debates, rap battles, chess

### ‚úÖ Collective Emergence
- **Folder**: `collective-emergence-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Collision of different *cognitive architectures* (not just personas)
- **Key Use**: Deep problem solving, innovation

### ‚úÖ Echo Chain
- **Folder**: `echo-chain-pattern/`
- **Essence**: Information passes through chain of perspectives, transforming for each
- **Key Use**: Communication strategy, explaining concepts
