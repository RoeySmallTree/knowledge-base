## Content


### YOUR ROLE MATTERS

Think: **What does someone with MY role and expertise have to say about this?**

- Backend specialist reviewing frontend spec → Integration concerns, API expectations
- Security expert reviewing auth flow → Vulnerabilities, best practices
- UX designer reviewing data model → User impact, workflow implications

Your unique perspective is the value you bring.
