## TLDR
We have split the prompt designs into separate files for easier management and usage.
-   **Common Context:** `V1_prompt_common.md` (MenuMaster use case, shared rules)
-   **Step Prompts:**
    -   `V1_prompt_bootstrap.md`
    -   `V1_prompt_plan_assign.md`
    -   `V1_prompt_inspect.md`
    -   `V1_prompt_peer_review.md`
    -   `V1_prompt_present.md`
    -   `V1_prompt_reflect.md`
    -   `V1_prompt_write.md`

## ELI5
Instead of one giant file, we now have small, specific files for each step of the AI team's process.
-   **Common:** The "Rulebook" and "Story" (MenuMaster).
-   **Bootstrap:** Starting the project.
-   **Plan:** Assigning tasks.
-   **Inspect:** Checking for safety.
-   **Review:** Teammates checking each other.
-   **Present:** Telling the user what happened.
-   **Reflect:** Thinking before doing.
-   **Write:** Doing the work.
